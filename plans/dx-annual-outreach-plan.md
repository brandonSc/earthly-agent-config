## Strategy

Reach out to a focused list of attendees via LinkedIn DM in the days leading up to the conference (April 9-15). The goal is to set up informal 1:1 coffee or small-group conversations while they're in SF, not a sales pitch at the conference itself.

**Angles:**
- Vlad is Earthly's CEO and will be in SF the week of the conference
- **AI angle:** As teams adopt AI-generated code at scale, they need guardrails to move fast without losing control. Lunar enforces engineering standards automatically across every commit via AI hooks and PR gates.
- **Standards angle:** Engineering teams have dozens of standards (testing, security, infra conventions, operational readiness) enforced through wikis, Slack reminders, and rubber-stamped checklists. Lunar turns those into automated guardrails enforced in PRs and AI coding workflows.
- Optional: mention that Twilio's platform engineering team uses Lunar (only if we decide to name-drop, see below)

**Name-drop option:** Jesse Adametz / Twilio is an existing user. Mentioning this adds credibility, especially with other platform eng leaders. However:
- Get Jesse's okay before using his name in outreach
- Alternatively, use "teams like Twilio" without naming Jesse personally
- Or skip the name-drop entirely and lead with the problem/product
